# English — Terms of Service

# Terms of Service — Stay@Maredumilli

*Last updated: 11 July 2026*

## 1. Who We Are
Stay@Maredumilli is a booking platform that connects customers with independently owned and operated hotels/stays in and around Maredumilli. We are a booking intermediary only. We do not own, manage, or operate any hotel property listed on this Platform.

## 2. Our Role
Our role is limited to displaying room availability, processing the booking/payment flow, and sharing the reservation with the hotel.

## 3. Hotel Responsibility
After check-in, room condition, cleanliness, staff conduct, amenities, food, safety, and all in-stay matters are the responsibility of the hotel owner/staff. Stay@Maredumilli does not mediate in-stay disputes.

## 4. Pricing
Prices are shown per room. The amount shown before payment is the booking amount for that room/date selection.

## 5. Occupancy
Each room has a maximum adult capacity shown on the listing. Guests must not exceed the listed adult capacity.

## 6. ID Verification
At least one guest must carry a valid Aadhaar card or accepted government photo ID at check-in. The hotel verifies this directly. Stay@Maredumilli does not collect or store ID documents.

## 7. No Cancellation and No Refund
All bookings are non-cancellable and non-refundable after payment. No refund is provided for any reason, including attempted cancellation, date change requests, no-shows, travel plan changes, weather, personal reasons, or hotel-side issues.

## 8. Check-in and Check-out
Check-in starts at 11:00 AM. Check-out is at 10:00 AM on the departure date.

## 9. Contact
For booking questions, contact us through Profile > Support.
